About us

There is no magic behind Stories from Zanna Photography ! .... Photography is our passion !!

Every wedding is a reflection of the love, hard work and dedication of the souls involved in it. Our endeavor is to convey these emotions in its natural bliss to every frame we capture. These frames speak great volumes beyond time and generations.
When others go behind trends, we at Zanna Photography strive hard to keep alive those precious moments without losing the happiness and intensity behind them.
Each and every frame has a story to tell and our Stories from Zanna Photography are the memories that we create for a lifetime.
